About ECOKISAN

ECOKISAN is an innovative platform designed to empower farmers with intelligent, data-driven solutions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technology, helping farmers improve crop yield, manage diseases, and make informed decisions to increase their profitability.

We leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning to provide features like **Pre-Crop Advisory**, which helps select the right crop based on soil and weather data, In-Crop Management for early disease detection, and Market Insights to ensure farmers get the best prices for their produce. Our goal is to make agriculture smarter, more sustainable, and more profitable for everyone.
